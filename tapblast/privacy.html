TapBlast Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you play TapBlast (the "Game"), developed by Huanda Tec.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our Game, including:

Device Information

When you access the Game, we may collect information about your mobile device, including your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information. This information is collected through the use of the Solar SDK and Max SDK.

Advertising Information

The Solar SDK and Max SDK we use in the Game may collect your device advertising identifier (such as the IDFA on iOS or the AAID on Android). This identifier is used for advertising purposes, such as serving personalized ads and measuring the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Game, including to provide and update features, content, and functionality.
  • To provide personalized advertising within the Game. The advertising identifiers collected by the Solar SDK and Max SDK help us deliver ads that may be more relevant to your interests.
  • To analyze how users interact with the Game, which helps us understand user behavior and preferences and make improvements to the Game experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third - party partners, including:

  • The Solar SDK and Max SDK providers. These partners use the information they receive in accordance with their own privacy policies to provide advertising and analytics services for the Game.
  • Advertising networks. We may share your advertising identifier with advertising networks to enable them to serve personalized ads to you.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
