About The Game

Tap Blast is an exciting block elimination game featuring adorable cat characters and challenging puzzles. Match colorful blocks, create powerful combos, and blast your way through hundreds of levels in this addictive puzzle adventure!

Game Features

Badass Effects

Experience stunning visual effects as you match blocks and trigger powerful combos. Watch as explosions light up the screen with every successful match!

Blazing Elimination

Create chains of matching blocks to trigger spectacular eliminations. The more blocks you match, the more impressive the elimination effects will be!

Classic Level Challenges

Progress through hundreds of increasingly challenging levels. Each level offers unique puzzles and obstacles to overcome with limited moves!
